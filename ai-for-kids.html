Fun AI Activities for Kids

Ready to get started with some fun AI activities? Here are a few ideas you can try at home:

  • Teachable Machine: Use Google's Teachable Machine to train a simple AI model to recognize images, sounds, or poses. It's a great way to understand how machine learning works.
  • Scratch AI Blocks: If your child is familiar with Scratch, they can use the AI extension blocks to create projects that recognize text, speech, and more.
  • AI-Powered Games: Play games like "Akinator" that use AI to guess a character you are thinking of. This can be a fun way to see AI in action.
